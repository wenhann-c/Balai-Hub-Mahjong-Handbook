Welcome to Balai Hub Mahjong Handbook
============================

This is the official *mahjong handbook* of the **Balai Hub**.

Welcome to the official **Balai Hub** Mahjong Handbook! This digital manual is designed to help club members master traditional Hong Kong Style Mahjong, from understanding tile basics to calculating complex table payouts.

   :align: center

.. note::
