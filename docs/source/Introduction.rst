====================================
Introduction to Balai Hub Mahjong
====================================

About Balai Hub
---------------
Welcome to Balai Hub! We are a community of mahjong enthusiasts dedicated to providing regular opportunities for players to meet, socialize, and spread the enjoyment of this classic game. Whether we are playing for points, chips, or purely for fun, the mahjong table is an exceptional place to connect and build friendships.

About This Guide
----------------
Mahjong is a tile-based game similar to Rummy that originated in China during the Qing dynasty. While there are numerous distinct regional variations across East and Southeast Asia, this club manual is explicitly tailored to the traditional **Hong Kong Style** ruleset practiced at Balai Hub.

Game Objective
--------------
The ultimate goal of the game is to be the first player to assemble a complete 14-tile winning hand. A winning hand—metaphorically referred to as a "dragon"—must be systematically organized into **four sets (melds)** and **one matching pair** of identical tiles known as the "eyes."

.. image:: images/hand_overview.jpg
   :alt: Structural overview of a winning 14-tile hand
   :align: center

While the first person to complete their hand wins the hand, the final payout or chip distribution is dictated by the complexity and beauty of the hand patterns, quantified in **Fan Points**.

--------------------------

   * - Mahjong / Complete Winning Hand

   * - Chow (Claiming a Discard to Form a Sequence)
     - 上 (*soeng*)
   * - Pong (Claiming a Discard to Form a Triplet)
     - 碰 (*pung*)

   * - Chicken Hand (A Baseline 0-Fan Hand)
     - 雞和 (*gaai wu*)
